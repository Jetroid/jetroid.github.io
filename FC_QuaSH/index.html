QuaSH - Quantised Sample and Hold.

 Originally developed to add more subsystems to my A Level Electronics Project, the first iteration was designed to co-exist with the Bartos Flur. As such, I used the Analogue inputs to scan each of the 8 inputs corresponding to the Bartos Flur outputs and decide which, if any, was high. (It made more sense to read the analogue inputs several times rather than waste components on a mixer.)

 I realised pretty early on that I had a problem; To Quantise, you want to have a total of 60 levels. As a PIC is binary, it is nicest to have 64 levels on 6 bits; hence, the upper 4 levels would be unused.
